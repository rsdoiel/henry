User Manual

Henry is a LLamafile (and GUFF) factory. It wraps the methods described on the Mozilla AI Llamafile website to make generating Llamafiles from HuggingFace.co hosted models nearly turn key. It is my hope this will encorage the use and exploration of apply Llamafiles to interesting use.
